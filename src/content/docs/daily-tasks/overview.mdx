As a new team member, you have a set of daily tasks to perform once you've logged in. These tasks ensure that you stay up-to-date with ongoing conversations, issues, and updates across various platforms. Here's a quick overview of what you need to do:

## Daily Task Documentation

<LinkCard title="Intercom" href="/daily-tasks/intercom/" description="Review and handle open conversations." />
<LinkCard title="Asana" href="/daily-tasks/asana/" description="Review and manage tasks in Asana." />
<LinkCard title="Slack" href="/daily-tasks/slack/" description="Check for messages or updates that need attention." />
<LinkCard title="Work Email" href="/daily-tasks/work-email/" description="Manage and respond to work emails." />
<LinkCard title="Research" href="/daily-tasks/research/" description="Conduct research as part of your daily tasks." />
<LinkCard title="Special Projects" href="/daily-tasks/special-projects/" description="Handle tasks related to special projects." />

1. **Intercom**: Review and handle open conversations.
2. **Asana**: Review and manage tasks in Asana.
3. **Slack**: Check for messages or updates that need attention.
4. **Work Email**: Manage and respond to work emails.
5. **Research**: Conduct research as part of your daily tasks.
6. **Special Projects**: Handle tasks related to special projects.

The following sections provide more detailed information on how to perform these tasks on each platform.
